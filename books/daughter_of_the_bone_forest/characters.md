# Characters

## Main Characters

### Rosy
{: #rosy }

**Rosamund "Rosy" Holt** -- The protagonist and narrator (most chapters). A 17-year-old bone familiar from a horse ranch in Forest's Edge. Has an unprecedented number of animal shifts: bone wolf (her most powerful, kept secret for most of the book), bone horse, mouse, raccoon, squirrel, otter, badger, and more -- over two dozen total. Has a unique bond with the sentient Bone Forest, which responds to her thoughts and emotions. Stubborn, fiercely protective of family, distrustful of authority, and terrified of being used as a military weapon. Struggles with survivor's guilt after [Guanyu](#guanyu)'s death and PTSD-like symptoms. Ultimately pledges to fight for [Shaw](#shaw) but refuses to be her queen.

### Shaw
{: #shaw }

**Shaw Colchuck** -- The princess of the Cursed Kingdom, called "Death's Heir." A bone witch and the Witch King's only child. Cool, controlled, and politically savvy, with a carefully maintained mask of indifference. A powerful necromancer who can raise skeletons from the dead. Falls in love with [Rosy](#rosy) but accepts (painfully) that Rosy won't rule beside her. Prophesied by every ice witch to be at the center of the next war. Her mother (the previous Familiar Queen) died in childbirth the same moment the Witch Queen (her grandmother) died, leaving Shaw to be raised by her father on the Frozen Mountain.

## The Holt Family

### Gran
{: #gran }

**Ylva the Red Wolf** -- [Rosy](#rosy)'s grandmother. A legendary former war hero and bone wolf familiar. Imprisoned in the Bone Forest with a magical cuff after attacking Thane Snass, believing [Pops](#pops) was murdered. Suffers feral episodes where her wolf mind overwhelms her human one. **Dies in the specter battle.**

### Pops
{: #pops }

**Otto Holt** -- [Rosy](#rosy)'s deceased grandfather. Former general under the Witch Queen, bone badger familiar. Officially died in an avalanche six years ago, but his ghost reveals he was assassinated on the Witch King's orders for discovering a secret plan to instigate the war with Vinland.

### Toketie
{: #toketie }

**Toketie Holt** -- [Rosy](#rosy)'s cousin, an ice familiar who shifts into an ice swan. Secretly one of the most powerful ice familiars at school. Was severely bullied in her early years at Witch Hall and survived by attaching herself to [Froya](#froya) Falk's social circle, sacrificing independence for safety. Has a fraught relationship with Rosy -- they split over social priorities but reconcile to some degree. Signs up for military service as a quartermaster.

### Other Holts
{: #other-holts }

**Papa (Sigmund), Mama (Ida), Uncle Inge, Uncle Chetwoot, Solemie** -- Rosy's immediate family at the ranch. Papa was apprenticed to a jarl's financial advisor. Uncle Inge is a former soldier. Solemie is Rosy's cousin engaged to Sapolill "Lilly" Klahn.

## Shaw's Entourage

### Aklemin
{: #aklemin }

[Shaw](#shaw)'s childhood companion from the Frozen Mountain. Has prophetic/foresight abilities ("consulting with the stars"). Wears a hybrid school uniform (being non-binary). Speaks in a slow, lazy drawl but gives eerily accurate warnings. Cryptic and wise.

### Einar
{: #einar }

A glass witch in [Shaw](#shaw)'s entourage. Kind, gentle, and talented with runes. Courts [Oluk](#oluk) with a brown leather necklace with gold runes. Already guaranteed the rank of thane.

### Yuyan
{: #yuyan }

A powerful flower witch and healer. The smallest member of [Shaw](#shaw)'s group. Secretly in love with [Shantie](#shantie). Apprentices at the school infirmary as her senior project. From Waiming.

## Key Students

### Oluk
{: #oluk }

**Oluk Blackwell** -- A glass familiar on scholarship. Son of illiterate obsidian miners. Shifts into a garter snake (first) and rattlesnake (second, which outranks all other senior glass familiars), and later a glass tortoise (third). Kind, earnest, looked down on by his classmates. Becomes [Rosy](#rosy)'s closest friend. Accepts [Einar](#einar)'s courtship. Senior project: redesigning the school stable with glass-witch enchantments.

### Charles
{: #charles }

**Charles Almstedt** -- A wealthy, entitled bone familiar. Shifts include deer, pronghorn, mountain goat, and bobcat. Antagonizes [Rosy](#rosy) throughout, bullying her during page duty and sabotaging her. However, he genuinely believes in [Shaw](#shaw) and eventually tells Rosy she's a fool for rejecting Shaw's love. Fights alongside them in the specter battle.

### Chao
{: #chao }

**Chao Hu** -- A powerful bone witch and necromancer. Son of [Shaw](#shaw)'s father's bone witch and familiar. Wide-built, serious. Bonded to [Jingyi](#jingyi). Friends with Shaw.

### Jingyi
{: #jingyi }

**Jingyi Wang** -- [Chao](#chao)'s bonded familiar. Gender-fluid (hair down = girl, hair up = boy). Shifts into a bone fox. Enthusiastic, warm, and politically astute. Tells [Rosy](#rosy) she could "lead all the familiars of this kingdom."

### Guanyu
{: #guanyu }

**Guanyu Cosho** -- [Shantie](#shantie)'s bonded flower familiar. Shifts into a king salmon (and later a frog). Son of the Cursed Kingdom's ambassador to Waiming. Charming, warm, dreams of a quiet life running a mail-order potions business. **Killed** by Vinlander terrorists in the Multah fire. His ghost asks [Rosy](#rosy) to tell Shantie to "swim upstream" and asks [Shaw](#shaw) to avenge him.

### Shantie
{: #shantie }

A senior flower witch bonded to [Guanyu](#guanyu). Developing a potion to cure feral episodes in familiars. Afraid of fire. Devastated by Guanyu's death; leaves school.

### Froya
{: #froya }

**Froya Falk** -- Leader of the senior ice familiars. Commanding, popular. Nobody crosses her.

## Teachers & Staff

### Madam Xu
{: #madam-xu }

Shapeshifting instructor from Daming. Has over a dozen shifts (first: python). Inspiring, believes familiars should push past their limits. Becomes [Rosy](#rosy)'s project advisor. Married to Mister Xu; "sister" to [Madam Bai](#madam-bai) (Xiaoqing).

### Madam Bai
{: #madam-bai }

**Xiaoqing** -- Survival Skills teacher. [Madam Xu](#madam-xu)'s adopted sister, also from Daming. Energetic, wild-haired, teaches through unconventional methods (like hiding in snake form for students to find).

### Madam Dyer
{: #madam-dyer }

Bone witch teacher (The Dead and Undead). Dark auburn hair. Hostile toward familiars, propagandist for the Witch King, refuses to help [Rosy](#rosy). Fled the Colonies. **Dies** when [Gran](#gran) attacks her during the Samhain ritual, becoming part of the specter.

### Mister Sorensen
{: #mister-sorensen }

**Gudmund** -- Ice witch, Court Etiquette teacher. Cruel to scholarship students. Performs palm readings and tarot that are genuinely prophetic.

### Madam Kawak
{: #madam-kawak }

Headmistress of Witch Hall.

### Mister Jostein
{: #mister-jostein }

Gruff, no-nonsense stable master. Married to Mister Voll.

### General Tepeh
{: #general-tepeh }

**Kiwa Tepeh** -- Commander of the Royal Company, sent by the Witch King to guard Witch Hall and begin military training. Gender-ambiguous, 23 years of service.
